She's Been Writing

female authors leading the charts on goodreads

Source: Goodreads — most read books in the UK over the last 12 months.

With International Women’s Day approaching, women dominate the current Goodreads charts, with female authors behind many of the books readers are adding to their shelves right now. The list spans genres, from fast-paced thrillers to expansive fantasy series.

A closer look at the rankings shows a clear romantasy takeover. We can’t help but notice Rebecca Yarros and Sarah J. Maas holding multiple spots at once: proof that readers are fully committed to dragons, fae politics, and emotional damage (the best kind). Maas has spoken candidly about the difficulty of writing these worlds, including on Call Her Daddy, where she connects the intensity of the work to her own therapeutic journey. The next chart zooms out to show how her series built momentum over time, from Throne of Glass to ACOTAR to Crescent City.
